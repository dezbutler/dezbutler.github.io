Temple Missionary Accommodations


Making Temple Inn and Suites your home away from home is easier than ever. With our specially designed extended stay rooms, you will enjoy all of the usual features and more.

Extended stay rooms feature a kitchenette furnished with a larger refrigerator and a full-size stove-top oven.

We know that when away from home giving of your time to service, you will have family that wants to visit, and so the living space provided is larger than other hotel rooms and features seating for guests.
