Target Audience

The data provided by the site should be easily understood by people of all types. While a specific type of user demographic may emerge, anyone who would be looking for weather info either on the fly or in preparation for travel are likely to have use for the site's info.

Scenarios

Where can I access the weather information from?

Weather Works is formatted for both desktop and mobile users, making it easy to both plan ahead and make decisions on the go.

Is this information coming from a meteorologist?

Online weather forecasts are based on a network of detection tools and weather radar pictures and models. The data generation is for the most part automated.

Does the site have information on my town?

Weather Works draws its information from national meteorological data, but the site’s focus is southeast Idaho, specifically the areas of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

Can I get weather information for a place that is outside of town?

The weather predictions will typically be fairly accurate for areas surrounding a specific city or town. Higher elevation areas outside of town will typically be cooler, and we will try to provide relevant info to those type of situations.

Can I get weather information for a different day?

Weather Works provides forecasts using meteorological data which typically extends to 10 days. No forecast is perfect, and conditions can change at a moment’s notice, but the information provided here is typically helpful in the overall view.
