Free Amenities

Breakfast: Included with every room reservation is a warm cooked breakfast served every morning in the hotel lobby.

Temple Shuttle: With locations so close, it is our pleasure to offer you free shuttle service to the temple through out normal operating hours.

Family History Center: As a specialized service, we are proud to offer our guests access to our own in-house family history libraries. As a guest, you will also have the privelege of consulting with our on staff family history experts.

Workout Room: Being away from home doesn't mean being away from your gym. Our main floor workout rooms will provide you with the exercise equipment you need to keep up with healthy habits even on vacation.

Indoor Pool: Whatever the weather may be, feel free to relax in our full-featured indoor pool. (Children must always be accompanied by an adult.)

High Speed Internet: You'll always be connected with our free guest Wifi networks.

Parking: Guests will never be charged for parking at any of our locations. We take measures to ensure that there is always ample space available when designing and developing our hotel grounds.

Changing Rooms: We know you're on the go, so we've provided changing rooms for our guests to use when on their way to or returning from a temple visit.

Additional Services

Child Care: As a special offer, we have child care facilities inside our hotels where parents can leave their young children for a reasonable rate.
