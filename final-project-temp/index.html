Temple Spotlight

The beautiful Calgary Alberta Temple was first announced on the 4th of October, 2008. Groundbreaking took place on the 15th of May, 2010. It was dedicated by President Thomas S. Monson on the 28th of October, 2012.

This temple features a gray granite exterior, made from finished blocks imported from China. The interior totals 33,000 square feet of floor area, and features a baptistry, two ordinance rooms, and three sealing rooms.

The Calgary Alberta Temple is the 8th temple built in Canada and the third temple built in Alberta. To book a stay near this beautiful location, visit our Reservations page to check rates and availability.
