Temple Reception Center and Catering


Receptions: At each of our hotel locations you will discover a reception hall that has been decorated to reflect the history and beauty of the nearby temple. Each center can be rented for groups large and small.


Catering: For reception groups, we offer world class catering to match the joyousness of the occasion. Dinner guests will be served excellent chef prepared entrees by our professional catering staff who will also attend to all your table decorating and waiting needs.
